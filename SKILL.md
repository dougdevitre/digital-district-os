# Digital District Operating System (DDOS) — SKILL.md

> The operational framework for building AI-native, cloud-first Digital Districts that replace legacy place-based innovation models.

## Purpose

This skill provides the complete operational playbook for planning, building, deploying, and scaling Digital Districts — decentralized, AI-powered ecosystems that give entrepreneurs instant access to tools, infrastructure, and intelligence without requiring physical location.

## Core Thesis

Legacy Innovation Districts (Cortex model) depend on real estate, institutional gatekeepers, geographic proximity, and slow public-private partnerships. Digital Districts (Delmar model) replace those dependencies with cloud infrastructure, AI intelligence layers, API ecosystems, and universal access. The shift is from **place to platform**.

## When to Use This Skill

Trigger for ANY request involving:
- Digital District planning, implementation, or scaling
- Innovation district modernization or transition
- City/corridor digital infrastructure strategy
- "Concrete to Cloud" or "Cortex to Code" frameworks
- Civic tech infrastructure, digital mainstreet, or smart corridor projects
- Government funding allocation (buildings vs. bandwidth)
- Cross-city interoperability for entrepreneurial ecosystems
- Stakeholder messaging for digital transformation initiatives
- Public-private partnership design for tech ecosystems
