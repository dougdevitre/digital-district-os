# Onboarding Playbook

## For New Partners Joining an Existing Digital District

### Week 1: Orientation
- [ ] Receive access to the repository and documentation
- [ ] Read: `00-framework/thesis.md` and `00-framework/stack-model.md`
- [ ] Complete the Readiness Scorecard for your node/organization
- [ ] Attend orientation session with operational team (1 hour)
- [ ] Identify your primary representative for the working group

### Week 2: Assessment and Alignment
- [ ] Complete asset inventory for your organization/node
- [ ] Identify 3-5 services you can contribute to the network
- [ ] Identify 3-5 services you want to access from the network
- [ ] Review governance model and identify your governance role
- [ ] Meet with Community Advisory Board liaison

### Week 3-4: Integration Planning
- [ ] Review API design principles and data sharing framework
- [ ] Identify technical integration requirements
- [ ] Draft Data Sharing Agreement with operational team
- [ ] Establish identity federation (if applicable)
- [ ] Set up development/staging environment access

### Month 2-3: Implementation
- [ ] Deploy API adapters for your contributed services
- [ ] Complete identity federation testing
- [ ] Sign Data Sharing Agreement
- [ ] Publish your services in the API catalog
- [ ] Attend first working group meeting as a member
- [ ] Nominate representative(s) for Community Advisory Board (if applicable)

### Month 3+: Active Participation
- [ ] Monitor your node's API health and usage
- [ ] Participate in monthly governance meetings
- [ ] Contribute to quarterly impact reporting
- [ ] Identify opportunities for cross-node collaboration

## For New Cities Deploying Their Own Digital District

### Phase 0: Preparation (1-2 months)

1. **Fork the repository** to your organization's GitHub
2. **Read the full framework** (00-framework/) — understand the thesis before acting
3. **Run the Readiness Scorecard** — establish your baseline
4. **Identify your path** using `01-assessment/decision-tree-start.md`
5. **Build your coalition** — minimum: 1 city champion, 1 community org, 1 technical partner
6. **Secure seed funding** — $100K-$500K for Phase 1 pilot

### Phase 1: Localize and Deploy (3-6 months)

1. **Localize all documentation** — replace St. Louis references with your city
2. **Select your corridor** using `03-decision-trees/corridor-selection.md`
3. **Customize messaging** for your local stakeholders
4. **Follow Phase 1 playbook** in `02-implementation/phase-1-street-to-system.md`
5. **Publish your district manifest** at your well-known URL

### Phase 2: Connect and Scale (6-18 months)

1. **Connect to existing districts** in your city (if applicable)
2. **Register with the Digital District network** by submitting your manifest
3. **Establish identity federation** with at least one other Digital District
4. **Follow Phases 2-4** in `02-implementation/`

### Ongoing: Contribute Back

1. **Document your experience** as a case study
2. **Contribute improvements** to the shared repository
3. **Share your metrics** via the standardized reporting format
4. **Help onboard the next city**
