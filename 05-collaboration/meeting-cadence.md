|---------|-----------|----------|--------|----------|

## Agenda Templates

### Operational Team Weekly Standup

```
DIGITAL DISTRICT — WEEKLY STANDUP
Date: [DATE]
Duration: 30 minutes

1. BLOCKERS (10 min)
   Each team member: What's blocking you?
   Action: Assign owner + deadline for each blocker

2. THIS WEEK'S PRIORITIES (10 min)
   - [Priority 1]
   - [Priority 2]
   - [Priority 3]

3. METRICS CHECK (5 min)
   - Wi-Fi uptime: ___%
   - Active users (7-day): ___
   - AI sessions (7-day): ___
   - Open support tickets: ___

4. QUICK DECISIONS (5 min)
   Any decisions that need the team but not the Board
```

### DD Authority Board Monthly

```
DIGITAL DISTRICT AUTHORITY — BOARD MEETING
Date: [DATE]
Duration: 90 minutes

CONSENT AGENDA (5 min)
- Approve previous meeting minutes
- Acknowledge reports received

1. EXECUTIVE REPORT (15 min)
   Operational team presents:
   - KPI dashboard review
   - Budget status
   - Key accomplishments
   - Escalated issues

2. COMMUNITY ADVISORY REPORT (10 min)
   Advisory Board chair presents:
   - Community feedback themes
   - Any veto actions or concerns
   - Upcoming community events

3. WORKING GROUP REPORTS (15 min)
   - Tech Working Group: 5 min
   - Policy Working Group: 5 min
   - Cross-cutting issues: 5 min

4. DECISION ITEMS (30 min)
   [List specific decisions requiring Board vote]
   For each:
   - Background/context
   - Options
   - Staff recommendation
   - Discussion
   - Vote

5. STRATEGIC DISCUSSION (10 min)
   [One strategic topic per meeting — deeper dive]

6. NEXT MEETING PREVIEW (5 min)
   - Key items for next month
   - Action items recap
```

### Community Advisory Board Monthly

```
COMMUNITY ADVISORY BOARD MEETING
Date: [DATE]
Location: [On-corridor venue]
Duration: 60 minutes

WELCOME + FOOD (ongoing — meeting starts on time)

1. COMMUNITY PULSE (15 min)
   Open floor: What are you hearing from neighbors,
   businesses, and community members?
   [Facilitator captures themes on whiteboard]

2. BOARD DECISIONS FOR REVIEW (20 min)
   [List any Board decisions that require Advisory review]
   For each:
   - Plain-language explanation of the decision
   - How it affects the community
   - Advisory Board discussion
   - Vote to approve, request changes, or veto

3. DATA & EQUITY CHECK (15 min)
   - Review equity metrics dashboard
   - Discussion: Are benefits reaching our neighborhoods?
   - Any data governance concerns?

4. COMMUNITY PRIORITIES (10 min)
   - What should the team focus on next month?
   - Any events, partnerships, or outreach needs?

NOTES published within 3 business days
```

### Quarterly All-Hands (Public)

```
DIGITAL DISTRICT — QUARTERLY ALL-HANDS
Date: [DATE]
Location: [Public venue on corridor]
Duration: 2 hours
Open to the public — no registration required

1. WELCOME + CONTEXT (10 min)
   Board chair: What is the Digital District, where are we

2. QUARTERLY IMPACT REPORT (20 min)
   Operational team presents:
   - KPI dashboard (live on screen)
   - Headline numbers
   - Equity breakdown
   - Highlights and challenges (honest)

3. ENTREPRENEUR SPOTLIGHT (15 min)
   2-3 businesses share their experience
   - What they built
   - How DD tools helped
   - What could be better

4. DEMO (15 min)
   Live demonstration of new tools or features

5. COMMUNITY Q&A (30 min)
   Open microphone — any question, any topic
   Board members and staff answer directly

6. BREAKOUT: FEEDBACK STATIONS (20 min)
   Stations around the room:
   - "What's working"
   - "What's not working"
   - "What do you wish existed"
   - "How can I get involved"

7. CLOSING + NEXT STEPS (10 min)
   - What we heard today
   - What we'll do about it
   - Next All-Hands date
```

## Meeting Norms

1. **Start on time, end on time.** Respect people's schedules.
2. **Decisions recorded in minutes.** Every decision captured with: what was decided, who's responsible, by when.
3. **Minutes published within 5 business days.** Transparency requires speed.
4. **No meeting without an agenda.** Agenda distributed at least 24 hours before.
5. **One conversation at a time.** No side discussions.
6. **Community meetings are accessible.** Childcare, food, translation, and virtual option provided.
7. **Disagreement is welcome. Disrespect is not.**
